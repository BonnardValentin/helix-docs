Class EpigeneticMarker

EpigeneticMarker - Environmental influences that can modify gene expression without changing the underlying DNA.

Epigenetic markers provide a mechanism for agents to adapt to environmental conditions without permanent genetic changes, allowing for flexible context-specific behavior.
